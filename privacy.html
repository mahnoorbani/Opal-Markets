Protecting Your Privacy at Opal Markets

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and security of your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as your name, email address, contact number, financial data, and trading activity. This information helps us verify your identity, process transactions, and provide a secure trading experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to create and manage your account, process deposits and withdrawals, verify your identity under KYC/AML policies, and communicate updates about trading activities, promotions, or system alerts.

Data Protection & Security

We use industry-standard SSL encryption, firewalls, and multi-layered authentication to protect your personal and financial data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information with regulatory authorities, payment processors, and legal entities when required by law or to fulfill trading operations.

Data Retention

Your personal information is retained only as long as necessary for business, regulatory, and compliance purposes. Once the retention period expires, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience, remember preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings from your browser at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. For any privacy-related requests, please contact our support team at support@opalmarkets.com.
